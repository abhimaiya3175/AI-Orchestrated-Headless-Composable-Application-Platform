# Hyderabad Travel Guide

## Overview
Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a major tech hub fondly called "Cyberabad" while also being famous for Biryani, pearls, and 400+ year old Nizami history. Excellent food city with a unique blend of modern tech parks and historic Char Minar old city.

## Best Time to Visit
- **October–February**: Best weather, 15–28°C, pleasant
- **March–May**: Hot (38°C+), manageable with planning
- **June–September**: Monsoon, less crowded, lower prices

## Top Attractions
- **Charminar**: 1591 mosque-monument, iconic symbol of Hyderabad, surrounded by Laad Bazaar
- **Golconda Fort**: Massive 16th century fortress known for acoustic features and diamond history — Koh-i-Noor came from here
- **Ramoji Film City**: World's largest film studio complex, can spend a full day here
- **Hussain Sagar Lake**: Heart-shaped lake with a 17.5m Buddha statue on an island
- **Salar Jung Museum**: One of India's largest one-person art collections (250,000+ objects)
- **Birla Mandir (Hyderabad)**: White marble temple on a hilltop, best viewed at sunset
- **Chowmahalla Palace**: Former Nizam's palace, beautifully restored, excellent museum

## Shopping
- **Laad Bazaar**: Glass bangles, traditional jewellery, attar (perfume) near Charminar
- **Hyderabad Pearls**: Visit GRT Jewellers or Mangatrai for authentic pearl jewellery
- **Shilparamam Crafts Village**: Art, handicrafts and cultural performances
- **Begum Bazaar**: Wholesale market, great for spices, dry fruits, hardware

## Food
- **Hyderabadi Biryani**: The city's pride — dum-cooked aromatic rice with meat, best at Paradise, Bawarchi, or Shah Ghouse
- **Haleem**: Slow-cooked mutton wheat porridge, especially popular during Ramadan
- **Mirchi Ka Saalan**: Green chili curry served with Biryani
- **Irani Chai & Osmania Biscuits**: Must-have at any old-city Irani café
- **Qubani Ka Meetha**: Apricot dessert, traditional Hyderabadi sweet
- **Double Ka Meetha**: Bread pudding soaked in milk and saffron

## Budget Guide
- **Budget**: ₹2,000–3,500/day (guesthouse + local food)
- **Mid-range**: ₹3,500–7,000/day (3-star + restaurants)
- **Luxury**: ₹7,000–20,000/day (5-star like ITC Kohenur or Taj Falaknuma)
- **Biryani meal**: ₹150–300 at good restaurants, ₹50–80 at local dhabas

## Transport
- **Hyderabad Metro**: Covers most tourist areas, fast and cheap
- **Auto-rickshaw**: Negotiate or use app-based (Rapido, Ola Auto)
- **Cab**: Ola and Uber widely available
- **Airport**: RGIA is 30km from city — factor in 45–90 min travel time

## Tips
- Visit Charminar in the evening for the best atmosphere and lighting
- Golconda Fort's Sound & Light show is held in evenings — book in advance
- Biryani lunch at Paradise Ameerpet or Hotel Bawarchi (A.C. Guards) is essential
- Ramoji Film City needs a full day — buy combo tickets at the gate
- Metro + auto combo is the most efficient way to get around
